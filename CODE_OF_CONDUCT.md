# Code of Conduct

Winderust contributors are expected to keep participation respectful,
constructive, and focused on the project.

Be considerate of different backgrounds and experience levels. Give actionable
technical feedback, assume good intent, and avoid harassment, discrimination,
personal attacks, threats, or publishing another person's private information.

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation
when behavior harms the community. Report conduct concerns privately through
the repository Security tab, or request a private contact channel without
posting sensitive details.
